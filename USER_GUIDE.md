# Battle of Wits - User Guide

## Introduction
Welcome to **Battle of Wits**, an interactive puzzle game inspired by the iconic scene from *The Princess Bride*. You play as the Dread Pirate Roberts (DPR) in a high-stakes intellectual duel against the villainous Vizzini.

## How to Play

### The Goal
Your objective is to deduce which of the two goblets is safe to drink from and which is poisoned. Since you have built up an immunity to iocane powder (represented by Hearts), you can survive one poisoned drink. Therefore, the game consists of a maximum of **2 rounds**.

### Game Structure
Each round is divided into two phases: the Riddle Phase and the Goblet Phase.

#### 1. Riddle Phase
- Vizzini will present you with **three riddles**.
- You must answer them (a single-word answer usually works best).
- **Gathering Clues:** This is the most critical part of the game. Vizzini's reactions to your answers contain oblique clues about the goblets:
  - **Correct Answer:** Vizzini will give a backhanded compliment. Embedded within this dialogue is an adjective that describes an attribute of the **safe goblet**.
  - **Incorrect Answer:** Vizzini will mock you with an insult. Embedded within this dialogue is an adjective that describes an attribute of the **poisoned goblet**.
- *Note:* Vizzini will never directly mention the goblets! You must pay close attention to the adjectives he uses.
- **Hints:** If you are stuck, you can ask Buttercup for a hint. Her hints are mechanically beneficial—they naturally contain extra adjectives related to the goblet attributes, giving you more clues to work with.

#### 2. Goblet Phase
- Gramps (the Narrator) will present two goblets (Left and Right) and provide a highly detailed description of their physical attributes.
- Your task is to match the adjective clues you gathered during the Riddle Phase to the descriptions of the goblets.
- Deduce which goblet is safe, and select it!

### Winning and Losing
- **Victory:** Choose the safe goblet in either round. Vizzini, having no iocane resistance, will drink from the poisoned goblet and be defeated.
- **Defeat:** Choose the poisoned goblet twice, exhausting your iocane resistance.

## Characters
- **Dread Pirate Roberts (You):** The protagonist solving the puzzles.
- **Vizzini:** The antagonist posing the riddles. His dialogue is the key to winning.
- **Buttercup:** Your helper. Ask her for hints if you need more clues.
- **Gramps:** The narrator who sets the scene and describes the goblets.
- **Boy:** The audience, providing meta-commentary and reactions from the framing story.

Good luck, and remember: never go in against a Sicilian when death is on the line!
