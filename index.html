⚔️ Battle of Wits ⚔️

You are the Dread Pirate Roberts, locked in a battle of wits against the cunning Vizzini — one goblet holds iocane powder, and only the clever survive. Answer Vizzini's riddles to earn clues hidden in his taunts, then choose your goblet wisely. Answer correctly and you win — answer wrong and your resistance to iocane may be all that saves you.
